Prepare_GEPAR Documentation

Pull gridded methane emissions maps for sectors not built with other functions from the Maasakkers gridded EPA inventory

Description

'Prepare_GEPA' writes 3 netcdf files of gridded methane emissions - 1 for thermogenic sectors, 1 for industrial landfills, and 1 for non-thermogenic sources.

Usage

Prepare_GEPA(inventory_year, input_directory, output_directory, domain)

Arguments

inventory_year

Numeric indicating the desired year of data to use.

input_directory

Character providing the full filepath to save/load input data

output_directory

Character providing the full filepath to save processed data

domain

SpatRaster providing the desired output grid, including the desired resolution and coordinate reference system

Details

This function downloads the most appropriate year of gridded methane data at 0.1 deg by 0.1 deg from Maasakkers et al. and pulls sectors that have not been calculated in other scripts. They are projected to the domain grid and combined into thermogenic and non-thermogenic groups, with the exception of industrial landfills which are saved separately. For thermogenic this includes

  • mobile combustion

  • Coal (abandoned, surface, and underground)

  • Petroleum Systems (exploration, production, refining, and transport)

  • Abandoned Oil and Gas

  • Natural Gas (exploration, processing, and production)

  • Industry (petrochemical, and ferroalloy)

And for non-thermogenic this includes

  • Composting

  • Enteric Fermentation

  • Manure Management

  • Rice Cultivation

  • Field Burning

The data is available at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.8367082. The file will be saved to input_directory to avoid re-downloading every run.

Value

Nothing is returned from the function, but the main outputs are 3 netcdf files of the methane emissions from the gridded EPA product. They are titled "GEPA_thermo.nc" for gridded EPA thermogenic, "GEPA_non_thermo.nc" for non-thermogenic, "GEPA_ind_landfill.nc" for industrial landfills.

Examples

library(terra)

grid_bbox=cbind(c(-76.65,-73.65),c(38.97,40.97))

grid_res=0.01

grid_crs="epsg:4326"

grid <- rast(nrows=diff(range(grid_bbox[,2]))/grid_res,

            ncols=diff(range(grid_bbox[,1]))/grid_res, xmin=min(grid_bbox[,1]),

            xmax=max(grid_bbox[,1]), ymin=min(grid_bbox[,2]), ymax=max(grid_bbox[,2]),

            crs=grid_crs)

Prepare_GEPA(inventory_year=2018,

             input_directory="~/../Desktop/",

             output_directory="~/../Desktop/",

             domain=grid)
